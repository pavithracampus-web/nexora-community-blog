Upwork vs Fiverr: Which Platform is Better for You?

A detailed comparison of the two biggest freelance platforms to help you choose where to start.

Sarah MillerApril 15, 2026
9.2K views 7 min read

Choosing between Upwork and Fiverr is one of the first decisions every freelancer faces. Both platforms have their strengths and weaknesses.

Upwork Overview

Upwork is known for higher-paying, more professional clients. You submit proposals and compete for jobs. Best for: Experienced professionals.

Fiverr Overview

Fiverr works on a "gig" model where you create service packages. Clients come to you. Best for: Beginners and service-based freelancers.

Key Differences

  • Upwork: Hourly & project-based, connects directly with clients, takes 20% commission
  • Fiverr: Fixed-price gigs, easier to get started, takes 20% commission

Pro Tip

Many freelancers use BOTH platforms to maximize their client reach.

Conclusion

Start with Fiverr to build reviews, then expand to Upwork for higher-paying work.
